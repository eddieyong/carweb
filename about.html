About CarWeb Malaysia

Your Trusted Partner in Finding the Perfect Second-Hand Car

Our Story

Founded in 2024, CarWeb Malaysia emerged from a vision to revolutionize the second-hand car market in Malaysia. We recognized the challenges faced by both buyers and sellers in the used car market and set out to create a platform that would make the process transparent, efficient, and trustworthy.

Our platform has grown to become one of Malaysia's most trusted online marketplaces for second-hand cars, serving thousands of satisfied customers across the country.

Our Values

Trust & Transparency

We believe in complete transparency in every transaction, providing detailed vehicle histories and honest assessments.

Customer First

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We strive to provide exceptional service and support at every step.

Innovation

We continuously innovate and improve our platform to provide the best car buying and selling experience.
